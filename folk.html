Folklore (stylized in all lowercase) is the eighth studio album by American singer-songwriterTaylor Swift . It was a surprise release on July 24, 2020, through Republic Records . Swift composed Folklore in quarantine during the COVID-19 pandemic as a "stream-of-conscious collection of songs and stories" from her imagination, and collaborated virtuallywith producers Aaron Dessner and Jack Antonoff .

The album was first announced by Taylor Swift on her social media just hours before its release on July 24, 2020, eleven months after her seventh studio album, Lover . Folklore was a surprise album released without prior promotion. The album, which contains 16 songs, was written and recorded in isolation during the COVID-19 pandemic. The production process involved a number of musicians, including Aaron Dessner , Bon Iver , and Jack Antonoff . The music video for the song "Cardigan", directed by Taylor Swift, was released simultaneously with the release of this album.

Upon its release, Folklore broke several sales records. The album was streamed on Spotify 80.6 million times in a single day, the highest number for a female artist. "Cardigan" reached number one on the Billboard Hot 100.and Folklore reached number one on the Billboard 200 album chart , making Taylor Swift the first artist to debut at number one on both the Hot 100 and Billboard 200 in the same week. The album also reached number one in other countries such as the United Kingdom , Australia , Finland , Norway , New Zealand , and Belgium .
